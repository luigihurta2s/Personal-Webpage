About Me

Education

I completed a Bachelor of Science in Information System with a Minor in Social Psychology from Park University, Parkville, MO. Throughout my academic journey, I maintanined a strong GPA. Park University's program provided me with a comprehensive understanding of systems, incluidng programming languages such as Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Python, Unix, and SQL. Additionally, I gained an insightful view into the Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC), where I familiarized myself with concepts dealing with system requirements gathering and analysis, system design, implementation and development, and system testing and deployment, among others.

Professional Background

After serving in the military for 8 years, I made the decision to go back and finish my studies. During my time in the military, I gained valuable technical experience in the field of telecommunications, networks, and electronic warfare. This hands-on experience laid the foundation for my interest in technology.

In my civilian life, I have continued to build upon my technical skills through various tech-related work. I have been involved in different projects that have further enhanced my understanding of technology and its applications in real-world scenarios.

Interests

Aside from my passion for technology, I have a keen interest in Social Psychology. I believe that understanding human behavior and interactions can greatly contribute to the success of any technological solution.
